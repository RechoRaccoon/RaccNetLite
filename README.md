# Free Open-Source Android First AT Protocol and e621 Media Viewer!
**How to use?**
- Log in with your Bluesky username and App Password, or swipe left to switch to e621 mode and sign in with your e621 username and API key, which can be gerneated on e621 in settings.
- Once logged in, swipe left and right to scroll to the next or previous post.
- Like, Save, Repost, Quote Repost, Download, Download as GIF, or Block the account with the buttons at the bottom of the screen, or by pressing and holding anywhere on the screen to open the radial shortcuts menu. You can also double tap to like.
- Swipe up to open comments.
- Swipe down to open settings. Swipe left or right in settings to switch between AT Protocol mode and e621 mode.
- Use the Share button in the radial menu to share the post with friends through DMs. and click the "From Friends" button in settings to see posts sent to you by your friends, along with the messages they attached to them.
- And so much more!!
